**OVERVIEW**

Our Virtual Reality (VR) School Tour offers an immersive and interactive way to explore the campus from anywhere in the world. Designed for prospective students, parents, and visitors, this VR experience provides a 360-degree walkthrough of key areas, including:
* Classrooms & Laboratories
* Library & Study Areas 
* Sports & Recreational Facilities
* Student Lounges & Cafeteria
* Auditorium

**KEY FEATURES**

- Immersive 360Â° Experience: Full 360-degree panoramic views of classrooms, labs, libraries, and outdoor spaces. And smooth navigation between different areas using hotspots.
- Interactive Hotspots: Clickable information points providing details about facilities, history, or achievements.
- Customizable User Experience: Multiple viewing modes (VR headset, desktop, mobile).
- Admission & Contact Integration: A "Contact Us" section for admissions inquiries. And integrated application links.
- Accessibility Features: Subtitles and text descriptions. And keyboard and mouse-friendly navigation for non-VR users.

**TECHNOLOGIES USED**

- WordPress: For user friendliness to navigate and use.
- Figma: For creating and managing design assets and prototypes.
- INSTA-360 X4 Camera: For capturing photos in 360-degree view detail with high quality pictures rendered.
